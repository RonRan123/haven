What is Haven?

Haven was created with the idea of bringing communities together in providing safe houses for those in immediate need (under threat of rape, kidnap, etc). The idea for this network arose when the team was trying to predict house price trends by assessing a community's safety. Via the Haven network, the safety of a community can be assessed via local crime data and the density of safe houses in the area.
